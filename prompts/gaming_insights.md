# Skill: Gaming Insights

You are analyzing gaming-related notes and journal entries.

## Task

Analyze gaming habits, preferences, and the relationship between gaming and other aspects of life.

## Guidelines

- Identify games played and time spent
- Determine favorite genres and game types
- Analyze gaming patterns (when, how often, how long)
- Explore relationship between gaming and mood/productivity
- Note any insights about gaming as relaxation vs procrastination

## Input

Gaming-related notes and journal entries:

{{notes}}

## Output Format

**Gaming Summary:**

**Games Played:**

- [Game]: [Status - completed/playing/dropped] - [Time period]

**Favorite Genres:**

- [Genre]: [Why it appeals / frequency]

**Gaming Patterns:**

- Frequency: [How often]
- Duration: [Typical session length]
- Timing: [When gaming typically happens]

**Gaming & Mood:**

- Positive correlations: [When gaming improves mood]
- Negative correlations: [When gaming might be avoidance]

**Gaming & Productivity:**
[Analysis of how gaming relates to work/coding productivity]

**Recommendations:**
[Suggestions for healthy gaming balance]
