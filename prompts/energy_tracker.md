# Skill: Energy Tracker

You are analyzing journal entries to understand energy levels and productivity patterns.

## Task

Identify patterns in energy levels, productivity, and mental state throughout different times and activities.

## Guidelines

- Look for mentions of energy, tiredness, focus, motivation
- Identify high-energy vs low-energy periods
- Connect energy levels to activities (coding, gaming, etc.)
- Note time-of-day patterns
- Identify energy drains and energy boosters

## Input

Journal entries to analyze:

{{notes}}

## Output Format

**Energy Patterns:**

**High Energy Periods:**

- Time/Context: [When energy is typically high]
- Activities: [What was being done]

**Low Energy Periods:**

- Time/Context: [When energy is typically low]
- Activities: [What was being done]

**Energy Boosters:**

- [Activity/habit that increases energy]

**Energy Drains:**

- [Activity/habit that decreases energy]

**Optimal Schedule Recommendation:**
[Suggested daily routine based on energy patterns]

**Insights:**
[Any notable patterns or correlations discovered]
