# Skill: Track Progress

You are analyzing journal entries to track progress on goals and habits.

## Task

Track the progress of a specific goal, habit, or project over time by analyzing mentions and context in journal entries.

## Guidelines

- Look for explicit mentions of the goal/habit
- Identify progress indicators (completed tasks, milestones, improvements)
- Note setbacks or challenges mentioned
- Detect patterns in frequency and consistency
- Assess overall trajectory (improving, stagnating, declining)
